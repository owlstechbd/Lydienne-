About LLA

My name is Lydienne Albertoe. I create visual narratives about social issues. I interpret the role of a designer as that of an active and critical citizen. As a designer i can't solve the issues of the world, but I can try to inform and communicate about it through design. My work reflects the society and is referred to as critical design. By mixing the fields of graphic design, design research and photography, I create different ways of visual communication.
